Recipe Catalog

Vanjaram Fish Fry

Vanjaram Fish Fry is a delicious and flavorful South Indian dish that highlights the taste of Vanjaram, also known as Seer Fish. This dish is popular for its crispy exterior and succulent, well-seasoned fish inside.

Mutton Biriyani

Mutton Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originates from the Indian subcontinent. It is a classic dish made by layering marinated and partially cooked mutton with partially cooked basmati rice. The layers are then slow-cooked or "dum-cooked" to perfection, allowing the meat and rice to absorb the rich spices and flavors.

Gulab Janum

Gulab Jamun is a popular and delectable Indian dessert that consists of deep-fried dumplings made from khoya (reduced milk solids) or milk powder. These fried dumplings are then soaked in a sugar syrup, imparting them with sweetness and a delightful, syrupy flavor.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a popular and iconic South Indian dish that has gained widespread popularity across India and globally. It consists of a thin, crispy rice and urad dal (black gram) crepe filled with a spiced potato filling.

Rasamalai

Rasamalai is a delectable and indulgent Indian dessert that hails from the Bengal region. It is a rich and creamy sweet dish made with soft paneer dumplings, which are soaked in a fragrant and saffron-infused milk syrup.

Badam Kheer

Badam Kheer is a delicious and rich Indian dessert beverage made with almonds (badam), milk, and sugar. It's a popular dish, especially during festivals and celebrations.
