Vanjaram Fish Fry

Ingredients

For marination:

  • 500 grams Vanjaram (Seer Fish) steaks
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

For coating:

  • 4 tablespoons rice flour
  • 2 tablespoons besan (gram flour)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

For frying

  • Oil for shallow or deep frying

Cooking Instructions

Cleaning: Clean and wash the Vanjaram fish steaks thoroughly. Pat them dry with a paper towel.

Marination: In a bowl, mix red chili powder, turmeric powder, ginger-garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, and lemon juice to form a smooth paste. Apply this marinade to the fish steaks, ensuring they are well coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Coating: In a separate bowl, mix rice flour, besan, red chili powder, and salt. Coat each marinated fish steak with the dry mixture. Ensure an even coating.

Frying: Heat oil in a pan for shallow or deep frying. The oil should be hot but not smoking. Carefully place the coated fish steaks in the hot oil. Fry each side until golden brown and crispy. Depending on the thickness of the fish, it should take approximately 3-5 minutes per side.

Drain and Serve: Once fried, place the fish on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess oil. Serve the Vanjaram Fish Fry hot with a wedge of lemon and your favorite chutney or sauce.
