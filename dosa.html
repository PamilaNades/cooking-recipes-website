Masala Dosa

Ingredients

For Dosa Batter:

  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 2 cups rice (preferably parboiled)
  • 1/2 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • Salt to taste

For Potato Filling (Masala):

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

For Dosa Topping:

  • Ghee or oil for cooking dosas

Cooking Instructions

Soak Ingredients: Wash urad dal and rice separately and soak them in water for at least 4-6 hours or overnight. Soak poha in water for about 30 minutes before grinding.

Grind Batter: Grind urad dal, rice, and soaked poha separately to a smooth batter using enough water. Combine the batters, add salt, and mix well. Allow the batter to ferment for 8-12 hours or until it doubles in volume.

Prepare Masala: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saute until onions turn golden brown. Add turmeric powder and grated ginger. Mix well. Add boiled and mashed potatoes. Season with salt. Cook for a few minutes, stirring to combine all the flavors. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Set aside.

Prepare Dosa: Heat a dosa tawa (griddle) and pour a ladleful of dosa batter in the center. Spread the batter in a circular motion to form a thin dosa. Drizzle ghee or oil on the edges.

Add Filling: Place a portion of the prepared potato masala in the center of the dosa.

Fold and Serve: Fold the dosa over the masala, creating a half-moon shape. Cook for a few more minutes until the dosa is crisp and golden brown.

Serve Hot: Serve the Masala Dosa hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
